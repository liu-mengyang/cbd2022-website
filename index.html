Call for Paper

2022 The Tenth International Conference on Advanced Cloud and Big Data (CBD 2022)

November 4-5, 2022, Guilin, China

Scope of Conference

The International Conference on Advanced Cloud and Big Data (CBD) provides a forum for both academics and practitioners working on cloud computing and big data technologies to explore new ideas, share their experiences and leverage each other's perspectives. Besides the latest research achievements, this conference also covers cloud/big data industry practices and their broad applications, and gives all participants a chance to identify the new/emerging “hot” trends in these areas. We solicit original papers on a wide range of cloud computing and big data topics that can be divided into three tracks but are not limited to:

  • Research Track
  • • Cloud Security and Privacy
  • • Cloud Data Center Networking
  • • Cloud Virtualization
  • • Cloud Storage
  • • Multi/Hybrid/Inter Cloud
  • • Big Data Processing/Mining/Query
  • • Big Data Security and Privacy
  • • Edge Computing and its applications
  • • Deep Learning/Federated Learning
  • • Cloud and Big data Management
  • • Blockchain and its applications
  • • Social Network
  • • Edge Computing Architecture and Algorithm
  • • Machine Learning and AI at Edge
  • Industry Track
  • • Cloud Computing Solutions
  • • Cloud Computing Specifications/Standards
  • • Cloud and Big Data in Industry
  • • Big Data in Smart city/Home
  • • Edge Computing Solutions
  • • Blockchain Solutions
  • Application Track
  • • Cloud based Industrial Internet
  • • Edge based Industrial Internet
  • • Mobile Edge Computing
  • • Machine/Deep Learning applications
  • • Social Networking
  • • Knowledge Graph
  • • Blockchain applications

Paper Submission

Submitted manuscripts must be formatted in standard IEEE US Letter Format and must be submitted via EasyChair as PDF files. The review version is limited to 6 pages (IEEE proceedings format), including references and illustrations. Submitted papers should not be previously published in or be under consideration for publication in another conference or journal. Submission of a paper should be regarded as an undertaking that, should the paper be accepted, at least one of the authors will attend the conference to present the paper. Please note that all accepted papers must be registered (with regular registration fee RMB ￥3600 and RMB ￥650/page for additional pages ) in order for them to be included in the conference proceedings.

Publication

All submitted papers will be reviewed by program committee members based on their originality, significance, relevance and clarity of presentation. Accepted papers will be published by Conference Publishing Services (CPS) and will be submitted for indexing to EI (Compendex). Authors of selected papers will be invited to submit revised and expanded version of their papers to be considered for publication in special issues of well-known international journals such as Concurrency and Computation: Practice and Experience (SCI), Tsinghua Science & Technology (SCI), Big Data Mining and Analytics (IEEE), Security and Communication Networks (Hindawi), etc.

Description

The Guilin Hotel is the one four-STAR HOTEL in Guilin which has passed ISO9001 and ISO14001 international quality and environmental system certification. It is located in the famous scenic area of two rivers and four lakes in the city center and within 10 minutes’ walk to the commercial district, several minutes’ walk to the Elephant Trunk Hill Night Market. It is 29 km (30 minutes drive) far away from Guilin liangjiang international airport and 1.5 km from the Guilin railway station respectively. There are many kinds of facilities such as free parking, Chess and card room, Table tennis room, swimming pool, fitness room, massage room or sauna. Credit Card including UnionPay Card and Matster Card are all supported. Guests can enjoy Western breakfast and Buffet breakfast at the hotel restaurant surrounded by ancient trees, stone tablets, pavilions and clear water, just like a beautiful picture. Guilin Bravo Hotel is ideal for business trips and holidays.
